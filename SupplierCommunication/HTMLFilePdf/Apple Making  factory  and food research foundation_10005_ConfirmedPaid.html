Welcome:

Ireland has it all for your vacation. From the haunting beauty of the pure, unspoiled landscapes and the drama of the coastline, to the urban buzz of the country's dynamic cities mixed with the magic of thousands of years' worth of culture and history, Ireland is a country that never fails to surprise.

Inclusion:

  • Blarney Castle
  • Blarney Woolen Mills
  • Cobh Heritage Centre
  • Cork City
  • Kinsale fishing town
  • Old Midleton Whiskey Distillery

Day 1 Wednesday, 05 Jul 2023 - George Best Belfast City Airport

12345

As you travel from Dublin to Cork, you’ll see some of the beautiful scenery that Ireland has to offer, so make sure to have the camera ready! You’ll get to kiss the Blarney stone along with finding some incredible Irish made items at the Blarney Woollen Mills. As well as seeing what Cork City has to offer, you’ll get to experience the old Midleton Whiskey Distillery. The Cobh Heritage Centre is also one of the highlights of this day as it is an amazing museum filled with rich history and you may even learn about, and trace, your ancestors.

Day 1 Wednesday, 05 Jul 2023 - George Best Belfast City Airport

12345

Get ready to experience the most stunningly beautiful scenic routes as you travel along the iconic Ring of Kerry. You will get to view what the Irish coast has to offer in terms of cliffs and beaches. You’ll see the amazing Killarney National Park in all its glory, along with visiting the Muckross House and Gardens. You’re in for a treat with a couple of demonstrations of true farm life in Ireland, which includes sheep shearing, and sheepdog herding flocks, all whilst learning about the different breeds of sheep from authentic Irish farmers.
